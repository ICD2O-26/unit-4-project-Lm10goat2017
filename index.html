Cloud Computing

What Is It?

Cloud computing means keeping your files on the internet, not on your computer. You use other people's big computers through the internet.

How It Works

You send your files to big computers called servers. Then you can open your files from any phone or computer with internet.

Examples

  • Google Drive
  • Netflix
  • Zoom
  • Spotify

Good Things

You can work from home. You can share files easy. You do not lose files if your computer breaks. Small companies save money.

Bad Things

No internet means no files. Big companies see your data. Computers use lots of power and hurt nature.
